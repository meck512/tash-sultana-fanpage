Tash Sultana


This Australian musician has had musical genius pouring out of their soul since they were old enough to hold a guitar. It’s no secret this multi-instrumentalist, singer, songwriter, and producer is one of the most original and creative musicians of our time. Beginning their career busking on the streets, Sultana was a sight to see showcasing their creative looping techniques. Years later Tash released "Jungle" on social media, recorded from their bedroom. The video immediately went viral with one million views in five days. Leading to the J Award for “Unearthed Artist of the Year”. That video debut of “Jungle” now has over one hundred million views and counting.
